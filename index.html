AI PING PONG GAME

Ping pong -- also know as table tennis and whiff-whaff -- is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball.









Created by Yesh


    INSTRUCTIONS


  1. Click Play!
  2. Use the arrow keys, WS, or your nose to move up and down your paddle. You may also use your mouse/keypad to move.
    3. \
  3. a. If the ball goes to the right of the black screen, you win the point.
    b. If the ball goes to the left of the black screen, the opponent (computer, rival, AI) wins the point.
  4. If the opponent gets four points total, game over.


    REMEMBER


  1. If necessary you need to pause or you need to restart, click Restart or close the Ping Pong tab.
  2. If you are still having trouble on how to play this game, or if you keep losing, click Restart, then click Instruction again
  3. W key is to move up and S key is to move down.
